## How to Become a Contributor

We welcome contributions from everyone! Here's how to get started:

1. **Read the [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md)** guidelines
2. **Find an issue** to work on or propose a new feature
3. **Submit a PR** following our process
4. **Get reviewed** and merged

Once your first PR is merged, you'll be added to this file!
